Wristbands

During your stay at AQUA Centrum, you need to wear a color-coded wristband. The wristbands are made from hypoallergenic, waterproof silicone, which makes them resistant and safe to use.

The wristband allows you to:

  • Enter the recreational area

  • Lock and open your locker

  • Get access to saunas

  • Track the time spent in the recreational areaet access to saunas

Make sure to always keep your wristband on you. The wristbands are not marked with numbers, so they can easily be mixed up.

Wristband colors

The wristband colors mean the following:

  • GREEN - women's changing room

  • YELLOW - men's changing room

  • RED - persons with disabilities

  • ORANGE and PURPLE - women's fitness changing room

  • BLUE - men's fitness changing room

The colors help our employees quickly identify where guests are heading and guide you in the right direction.

Bright-colored wristbands improve your visibility in water. This makes the swimming pool area safer, and helps our lifeguards keep track of all guests.
