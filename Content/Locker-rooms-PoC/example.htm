Example

Your customers want to be sure their clothes and personal belongings are securely stored during their stay. That is why it is important to clearly explain how to use the lockers.

Current issues

The 'worth-to-know' page contains a large block of mixed information, including where the reception is, where customers can leave their coats and shoes, where and how to pay (but without a link to the price list), what the wrist straps colors mean, and how to use the tanning beds. Looking at the locker room access, the following issues arise:

  • The image of the wrist straps is outdated.

  • The image only shows four straps, but the description lists six different coloured straps.

  • According to the description, the wrist strap allows users to lock and unlock the lockers, as well as access and pay for the stay, but there are no instructions on how to do all this.

  • The instruction inside the locker room is rather general and mixed with additional reference information:

    Current instructions for closing and opening the lockers

    [text in image]:

    Closing and opening the locker is done by placing the strap's transponder to the lock.

    As a rule, the first free locker shall be chosen.

    After taking your items out of the locker, you shall leave the locker door open.
