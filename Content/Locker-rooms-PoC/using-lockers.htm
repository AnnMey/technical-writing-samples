Using the lockers

Use your wristband to easily lock and open your locker. The wristband also allows you to check your locker number.

For more information, see Wristbands.

If your wrist strap does not work as expected, the receptionist can provide you with another one.

How to lock the locker

You can choose any locker that is free. Free lockers should have the door open.
We recommend choosing the first locker that is free.

Use your wristband to lock the locker door.

  1. Close the locker door.

    When the door is not locked, the lock is pushed out.

  2. Press the wide part of your wristband against the lock to push the lock in.

  3. Check if the door is locked.

    When the door is locked, the lock is pushed in.

How to open the locker

Use your wristband to open the locker door.

  1. Press the wide part of your wristband against the lock.

    The locker door opens, and the round lock is pushed out.

  2. Open the locker door.

How to check my locker number

Use your wristband to check your locker number.

  • Tap your wristband against the electronic reader.

    The reader displays your locker number.

Electronic readers are located between the changing rooms and the showers.
